THE PROBLEM

Waste Crisis

Millions of tons of plastic and organic waste cannot be efficiently recycled.

Limited Home Solutions

Households and small communities lack safe, convenient ways to convert waste into usable energy.

Industrial Limitations

Large-scale pyrolysis plants face safety, efficiency, and emissions limitations.

400M+ Tons

Of plastic waste each year with less than 10% recycled effectively. Incineration plants release 400M tons of CO₂ yearly.

OUR SOLUTION

Industrial Plants

Buy/recycle plastics from plants or households → produce eco-friendly fuel using advanced microwave pyrolysis.

Home Units (R&D)

Impact

Reduces landfill waste, produces low-carbon fuel, enables circular economy at multiple scales.

HOW IT WORKS

Understanding Microwave

Pyrolysis is thermal decomposition in the absence of oxygen. We use microwave energy to rapidly heat waste, converting it into vapor and then condensing into fuel.

Stage 1: Heating & Decomposition

Microwaves heat materials to ~500°C; bonds break and vapors form.

500°C

Stage 2: Condensation & Collection

Vapors move to the condenser, cool and form liquid fuel which is collected.
