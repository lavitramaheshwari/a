The Science of Pyrolysis

Pyrolysis is a sustainable thermal decomposition process that converts waste materials into valuable products without combustion. By heating biomass or plastic waste in an oxygen-free environment, we transform waste into biochar, bio-oil, and syngas—creating a circular economy solution.
